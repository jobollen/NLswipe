This melody-driven hardcore band from Belgium has been touring non-stop since these youngsters started out in 2011. After spreading their name across the country and after releasing a first EP they are now climbing their way up. In november 2013 the band released another promising EP which had an immense impact and made them cross the country borders. Their sound has become heavier and they bring the most energetic live show you’ve probably ever seen. April 2015, they released there first full-length album 'Hopes & Dreams'. With this album, they continued spreading there message across Europe. After that, in June 2016, a brand new single 'Heartless' was released. It’s been a crazy journey so far but there’s a lot more to come.
